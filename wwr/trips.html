Half-day and full-day excursions

Half-Day Excursions Trip Details

  • Duration: 4-5 hours
  • Distance: 10-15 km
  • Difficulty: Class II-III rapids (moderate)
  • Age Restriction: 8+ years old

Itinerary

  • Check-in and safety briefing at our riverbase
  • Guided rafting trip down the river
  • Scenic stops and swimming opportunities
  • Return to riverbase for snacks and debriefing

Full-day Excursion Trip Details

  • Duration: 8-10 hours
  • Distance: 25-30 km
  • Difficulty: Class III-IV rapids (challenging)
  • Age Restriction: 12+ years old

Itinerary

  • Check-in and safety briefing at our riverbase
  • Guided rafting trip down the river
  • Scenic stops, swimming opportunities, and picnic lunch
  • Return to riverbase for snacks and debriefing
