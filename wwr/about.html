About White Water Rafting

News

White water rafting is a thrilling and challenging recreational outdoor activity using an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other bodies of water. This is often done on white water or different degrees of rough water, in order to thrill and excite the raft passengers. The development of this activity as a leisure sport has become popular since the mid-1970s. It is considered an extreme sport, as it can be dangerous.

History

White Water Rafting started in the 1970s and has grown in popularity since then.
